Experience the breathtaking scenery around the Dingle Peninsula via car, bicycle or on foot. We are sure you will be mesmerized by its outstanding beauty. The Skellig Islands invite intrigue as they pertutrude sharply from the bounding sea. So awesome are the Skelligs that they were the backdrop and filming location for the blockbuster film, Starwars Episode VII. It is claimed that the first monk on the Skellig Michael Islands was Saint Finnian of Clonard. Considered by by many as a father of Irish monasticism, Saint Finnian who it is also said almost all of the birds of Ireland gathered soon after his birth at the end of 5th Century as a sign of the holy life he would lead used much of his time preaching in Wales until he later returned to his homeland and found churches most noticeably at Great Skellig which is the larger among Skellig Michael Islands.

With Skellig Michael Cruises one can view the Skelligs before landing and exploring. Book by calling Paul Devane on 087 6178114. Enjoy!!

Fáilte go Mara Beo an Daingean!! Oceanworld Aquarium Dingle is a great family day out in Kerry, please consider booking in advance. Oceanworld Aquarium is located in Dingle, County Kerry and is Ireland’s largest aquarium. Our Aquarium hosts a collection of magnificent Sand Tiger Sharks, Gentoo Penguins, Asian Short-Clawed Otters and includes an abundance of different fish species. Experience this enchanting underwater world and learn the wonders of the ocean.
