History

Waterford is the oldest city in Ireland and Vikings established a settlement here in 853.Throughout the medieval period, Waterford was Ireland's second city after Dublin.

Beach Activities

There are over 30 beaches along the Waterford Copper Coast. This is now a UNESCO Global Geopark. It extends along the County Waterford coastline and is named after the 19th century copper mines.
