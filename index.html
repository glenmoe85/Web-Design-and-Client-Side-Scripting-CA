Cead mile failte is a well-known greeting world wide, It welcomes visitors to our shores one hundred thousand times. Our hospitality is world famous, friendly hellos, helpful strangers and being welcomed by the locals is par for the course here. Ireland is renowned for it’s beautiful landscapes and it’s wonderful hospitality. A land of adventure and majestic scenery, you will be spoilt for choice each day. The four provinces; Leinster, Munster, Ulster, and Connaught all offer a wide variety of scenes to see, mountains to conquer, pubs to visit and sporting events to enjoy as a spectator or indeed, to compete in. Learn about the dark history of the famine days and see the physical reminders that live to this day in the famine walls and paths around Kerry and Cork. Learn of the mighty and Feared Vikings that ruled over Dublin on the Dublina Viking Tour. Explore our musical and food festivals many travel far and wide to experience. Engage with our national sports and watch our champion rugby team play with pride at The Aviva Stadium, where drum beats and heartfelt song creates an electric atmosphere that can move the soul and evoke emotions out of the surliest man. Ireland is abundant with opportunities to partake in water sports of all descriptions; surfing on the west coast, kite surfing on the east coast, kayaking and sea swimming are favourite past-times of our people and we welcome you to join us. Our creativity and craft industries are second to none and never fail to amaze; visit Waterford Crystal for exemplary exquisite craftsmanship or the plethora of artists of all genres who are dotted around our isle, who produce amazing work and are the backbone of our cultural experience.

This website has been developed to showcase what the counties of Ireland have to offer, and to inspire your curiosity about this wonderful place we are lucky to call home. You can sign up to newsletters so you don’t miss any of our exciting events and opportunities, get a taste of what Ireland is like by video, photographs and links to various offerings that you certainly don’t want to miss!
